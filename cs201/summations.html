Prove ∑ k = 1 n k = n ( n + 1 ) 2 .

By induction on n.

Basis: Let n = 1.

∑ k = 1 n k = n ( n + 1 ) 2

∑ k = 1 1 k = 1 ( 1 + 1 ) 2

1 = 1 × 2 2 = 2 2

1 = 1

∎
