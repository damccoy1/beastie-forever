RE: Exam question discussion - lusth - 09-10-2018

(09-10-2018, 07:26 PM)Jake.Cloud Wrote: For question 20, why would the time complexity be n instead of nlog(n)?

Since the loops are not nested, their compexities are added to achieve the cummulative complexity. Since n dominates log(n), the overall complexity is linear.
