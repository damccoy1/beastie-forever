So I am trying to wrap my head around the DA union function, and how to both transfer from the donor in amortized O(n) time without doing this:
      insertDA(recipient, recipient->size, removeDA(donor, donor->size - 1));
Which flips the order of the donor array.
If I do this: 
      insertDA(recipient, recipient->size, removeDA(donor, 0);
or this:
for (i = 0; i < donor->size; i++)
      insertDA(recipient, recipient->size, removeDA(donor, i));

it runs at O(n^2) time, since both would shift the entire donor array by n every removeDA, and it would take n iterations to empty the donor.

If there is a third strategy I am missing, or if I am misunderstanding one of the examples I have given, I would love to know it.
