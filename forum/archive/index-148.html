In the da page one of the assertions is "removeDA - The size shall be greater than zero." Does this mean we can never remove everything from our array? Or does it mean size must be greater than or equal to 0, and capacity must be greater than zero?

andrasveto

09-09-2018, 01:52 AM
When you call the remove function, there must be something to remove. That is, the size of the array must be greater than zero. If you call remove on an array with one element, then the size is greater than zero, and after the removal is completed the size is zero.
