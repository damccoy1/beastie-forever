It is case 2 because :

n^logba = n^log base 4 of 1 = n^0 = O(1)

and think of f(n) = n^0 = O(1).

So, f(n) = n^logba

Therefore, case 2 follows.

And T(n) = n^logba * logn = O(1) * log(n) ---> so, T(n) = O(logn)
