  Beware
Posted by: lusth - 10-09-2018, 10:58 PM - Forum: Project 1 - No Replies

A couple of students were getting different mazes that what the test1 dropbox indicated.

This was because they only pushed a cell onto the stack only once. If you pop a cell and it has more than one eligible neighbor, it needs to pushed back onto the stack before the chosen neighbor.

Or you could peek, count neighbors, push a neighbor if any exist, otherwise pop.
