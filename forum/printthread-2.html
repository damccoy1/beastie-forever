RE: Passing free functions to setSTACKfree/setQUEUEfree - sbcarp - 10-11-2018

Try not to pass freeMAZE to setSTACKfree or setQUEUEfree.
You don't need to free the items in stack or queue, just free stack and queue themselves.

freeMAZE should free all cells and then call freeStack and freeQueue.


BTW, change my MAZE * to void * could fix incompatible problem.
