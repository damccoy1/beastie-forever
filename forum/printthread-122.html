RE: CDA first insertion - lusth - 09-04-2018

I started my indices both at zero and treat the first insertion as a special case. I assume the back index always points to the last valid element in the filled region, so having both indices pointing to zero after the first insertion is OK (under my assumptions).

You could also simply compute the back index from the front and then you only have one index to keep track of. That would probably be better than what I did.
